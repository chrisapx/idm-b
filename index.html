What powers our apps

IntelligenceKit

The agent layer behind our AI features. Orchestrates models, handles memory, powers interactions from subtle prompts to complex autonomous workflows. Built for real products, not demos.

FeatherKit

Our design system. Components, patterns, and primitives that make apps feel consistent from day one. Born from our own products, refined with every release.

RevenueKit

Subscription management done right. Handles pricing experiments, grace periods, and cross-platform purchases without making you think about StoreKit.

InsightsKit

Privacy-first analytics. Track what matters without sending user data to our servers. All processing happens on-device.

FitKit

The health intelligence behind Peaks and Whistle. Sleep stages, training load, recovery metrics, circadian rhythm, all processed on-device, all ready to use.
